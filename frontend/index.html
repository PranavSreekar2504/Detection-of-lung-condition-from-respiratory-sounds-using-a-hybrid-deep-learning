Detectable Conditions

RespiCare can identify the following respiratory patterns

✓ NORMAL
Normal
No significant lung condition detected. Respiratory sounds are within normal range.
⚠ ABNORMAL
Asthma
Chronic airway inflammation causing breathing difficulty and wheezing.
⚠ ABNORMAL
Pneumonia
Lung infection causing fluid/pus accumulation in air sacs.
⚠ ABNORMAL
COPD
Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease causing airflow limitation.
⚠ ABNORMAL
Bronchitis
Inflammation of bronchi tubes causing persistent cough.

COVID-19
Novel coronavirus infection affecting the respiratory system.
